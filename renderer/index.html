Features

Everything you need to run a resell operation — tracking, monitoring, and market research in one place.

Home Dashboard
  • Live calendar with upcoming release dates you've added
  • Profit overview chart at a glance
  • Pinned notes for reminders and drop tips
Sales
  • Log sales with platform, price, fees, and photos
  • Auto-calculated profit per sale
  • Running profit chart on home screen
  • Filter and sort your full sales history
Inventory
  • Track everything you own but haven't sold yet
  • Purchase price, condition, and photos per item
  • See total capital tied up in stock at a glance
Packages
  • Track shipments by tracking number and carrier
  • Live tracking events pulled without leaving the app
Market Lookup
  • Search any item and see live eBay active or sold listings
  • Instantly research resell value before you buy or list
Monitors
  • Nike SNKRS — watches the launch feed, shows drop method (Draw / FCFS), live countdown per card, and available sizes. Only shows drops within 2 hours of launch so feed stays relevant
  • Funko — scrapes Funko's site for new products and restocks. Alerts on restock or price drop
  • Shopify — monitors any Shopify store (Supreme, Kith, etc.) for new products or restocks
  • Best Buy / Amazon / LEGO — stock monitors on specific product URLs
  • Aggressive in-app Nike alert — sound × 3, window flash, persistent toast with size buttons and ATC links
  • Boost mode — auto-tightens Nike polling to every 10s within 5 min of a drop
  • Discord webhook pings with rich embeds for every monitor type
  • Nike Discord embed includes clickable size-to-cart links and StockX/GOAT search buttons
Discord Integration
  • Selfbot keyword alerts — monitors channels you're in and fires an in-app alert when any keyword matches (e.g. restock, ATC, cop)
  • Cook Group Feed — subscribe to any channel by ID and every message posts to a live feed inside the app, no keywords needed. ATC links are highlighted automatically
  • Outgoing webhooks push monitor alerts to your own Discord server
Proxies
  • Add, test, and manage a proxy list
  • Test all proxies at once or individually
  • Dead proxy cleanup with one click
Phone Alerts (Pushover)
  • Push notifications on your phone for all monitor alerts, Discord keyword matches, and cook group feed messages
  • Tap notification to open buy/ATC link directly on your phone
  • Custom sounds per alert type (live/restock vs new product)
  • Emergency mode — live drop alerts bypass Do Not Disturb
System Tray
  • Minimize or close hides the app to your system tray — keeps running in the background
  • Double-click tray icon to restore, right-click for Quit
  • Monitors, selfbot, and phone alerts all stay active when minimized
